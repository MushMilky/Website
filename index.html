What are Milky Mushrooms?

Milky mushrooms (Calocybe indica) are a large, edible tropical mushroom native to India and Southeast Asia. They are nutritious, low in calories and fat, and offer a range of health benefits.

  • High in protein — great for vegetarians & vegans
  • High dietary fiber — supports digestion
  • Rich in vitamins (B-complex) and minerals
  • Contains antioxidants and beta-glucans that support immunity

Nutrition (per 100g)

  • Calories: 30–35 kcal
  • Protein: 3.1 g
  • Carbohydrates: 4.5 g
  • Fat: 0.3 g
  • Fiber: 2 g

Tip: Avoid overcooking and use minimal oil to preserve nutrients.
